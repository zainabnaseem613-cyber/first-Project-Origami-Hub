However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. Usage data is typically retained for internal analysis for a shorter period.
